Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
1. Introduction

Welcome to MathIQ Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use MathIQ Pro:

  • Username & Email — used to create and manage your account
  • Game Data — scores, XP, streaks, badges, and game history
  • Profile Picture — if you choose to upload one
  • Usage Time — daily practice minutes tracked locally on your device
3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your player account
  • Display leaderboards and player profiles
  • Track your progress, XP, and achievements
  • Enable multiplayer game features
  • Send optional practice reminders (only if you enable them)
4. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Game progress and usage time are also stored locally on your device using browser storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your username and score may be visible to other players on the leaderboard — this is required for the multiplayer and ranking features.

6. Advertising

MathIQ Pro may display ads via Google AdMob. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to show relevant ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Children's Privacy

MathIQ Pro is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Update your profile information at any time
